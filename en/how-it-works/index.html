From “I would like to do that” to “Come on, let's do this”.

Step 1

Discover activities that fit your day.

Browse what’s happening nearby — by category, distance, and time.

Browse the feed

  • See what’s happening nearby.
  • Spot activities that match your interests and schedule.
  • Open an activity to view details and participants.

Outcome: you know what’s happening and what to expect.

Switch to the map

  • Discover activities visually around you.
  • Quickly understand distance and location.
  • Pick what fits your day with less guesswork.

Outcome: you find the right activity faster.

Step 2

Join and coordinate with clarity.

Request to join, check the details, and coordinate in chat — no awkward guessing.

Open activity details

  • Check time, place, and participation limits.
  • See who’s already in.
  • Request to join (or accept requests if you created it).

Outcome: you’re part of a real plan — not vague intentions.

Coordinate in the activity chat

  • Ask questions and clarify expectations.
  • Confirm last details (meeting point, timing, etc.).
  • Keep it simple and respectful.

Outcome: everyone knows what’s happening.

Step 3

Meet in real life — the app stays in the background.

Do the activity together. Connections happen naturally through shared experiences.

Show up with clear expectations

  • The activity is the reason to meet — simple and clear.
  • No pressure, no performative “dating vibe”.
  • You can always leave or reschedule respectfully.

Outcome: real connections — without pressure.

Profiles stay lightweight

  • Interests and context — not dating bios.
  • Less performance, more authenticity.
  • Built to support meetups, not endless scrolling.

Outcome: the app helps you meet — then gets out of the way.

Features

What you can do in ActiFly.

Short, practical, and focused on the experience.

Planning & discovery

  • Create activities with time, location, category, and limits.
  • Discover activities via map and feed.
  • Use filters to find what fits your schedule.

Connection & safety

  • Activity-based group chats to coordinate.
  • Profiles with interests (context, not performance).
  • Built-in safety tools like reporting, blocking, and moderation.
Positioning

ActiFly is not a dating app.

It’s designed for shared activities, hobbies, and social experiences — with clear expectations and respectful interactions.

Activity-first

The activity is the reason to connect — not endless profile browsing.

Human, not performative

If something more grows naturally, that’s human — but it’s never the goal of the platform.
