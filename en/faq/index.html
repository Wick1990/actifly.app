FAQ

Your questions – answered.

Clear answers about ActiFly, how it works, and what to expect.

Is ActiFly a dating app?

No. ActiFly is not a dating app. It’s designed for shared activities, hobbies, and real-world meetups — without romantic pressure or dating mechanics.

What can I use ActiFly for?

You can discover activities near you, join others for shared experiences, or create your own activities — from walks and sports to games or local events.

Is ActiFly free to use?

Yes. ActiFly is free to use. Optional features may be added in the future, but the core experience will remain accessible.

Who can create activities?

Any verified user can create activities. Activities include clear details such as time, location, category, and participant limits.

How safe is ActiFly?

ActiFly includes multiple safety features such as email verification, Google authentication, reporting and blocking tools, and a moderation system.

You can learn more on our Safety page.

Can I block or report users?

Yes. You can block users at any time, which immediately removes contact. Reports are reviewed according to clear community rules.

How does moderation work?

Reports are reviewed carefully and handled according to community guidelines and applicable laws. Decisions are based on clear rules, not personal opinions.

Is my data protected?

Only necessary data is collected. Contact details are not shared publicly, and your data is handled in accordance with privacy laws.

Is there an age limit?

Yes. You must be at least 16 years old to use ActiFly.
